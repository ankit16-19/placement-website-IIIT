It works to train the students with effective problem solving ability, personality improvement as well as good communication skills. We are a team comprising of placement executives, students and faculties.”

Training and Placement Cell

“IIIT Bhubaneswar will be a global voice in the near future. The voices which will make the institute touch new technical heights and success. I am very proud to be a part of such an institute. Students here are trained though various stages before they are made to face the real world. Rigorous laboratory practices have made their fundamentals clear in most of their subjects. I wish they succeed in every field their life. ”

Prof. Harish Kumar Sahoo, Asst. Professor

“IIIT Bhubaneswar is dynamic, engaged in research, all round student's development and technological innovation. It provides an opportunity for exponential talent growth. The students of IIIT can be a pride of any prestigious academic institution. They are hard working, creative and always ready to face challenges. I am sure they can be an asset of any professional organization. ”

Prof. Suvendu Rup, Asst. Professor

“Our esteemed team of faculties make sure that the coverage of course is always deep, so that a student can take the next leap into turning theories to ideas.
Over these years our batch has produced an excellent team of students who have displayed proficiency in areas of programming, software-design, networking and web development. We have tried braving all odds by conducting our college's inter college Technical-Cultural festival Advaita which brought forth an array of new ideas in forms of innovative problem-statement based competitions. I surely hope that students of Batch 2017 can be valuable assets for any organization they will be joining.”

Students of 2017 Batch

“The T&P cell, IIIT-Bhubaneswar is committed to providing successful placement opportunities to its students. It coordinates the placement activities of the institute and also acts as a liaison between the companies, the institute and the students. In addition to providing placement support in various prestigious organizations, it also facilitates training of students in the industries during summer and winter vacations, institute-industry interactions, participation in seminars and conferences, conducting industry visits and inviting distinguished speakers to the campus. The cell has been successful in fulfilling its objective of making the institute as one of the most favoured destinations for hiring fresh talent from the campuses.”

Jolly Dey, Asst. Professor

“IIIT-BH is an autonomous university which offers B-TECH, M-TECH, PhD programmes. It has become the lucrative pool of talented brains. Our students are achieving in reputed programming contests and many other national competitions every year. They are well placed in many companies starting from Mind Tree to Mind Fire Solutions and many other multi national companies. They are doing excellent in their domain continuously which has attracted the attention of big recruiters to our campus.
The growing trend of recruitment is well visible, the placement record is remarkable and the average package per student is also increasing 20-25% annually”

Pushpanjali Mahapatra, Asst. Professor
