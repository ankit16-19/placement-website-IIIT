  • Position Offered: Software Developer
    IIIT Bhubaneswar was highly honoured when Snapdeal visited the campus for placements this season.In a one day long process snapdeal successfully recruited one of our students. The placement process included five rounds:
    1. Online Aptitude Test
    2. Three rounds of Technical Interview
    3. HR Interview abd Results declaration
    Students from CSE & IT were eligible to participate in the placement drive.
    No. Of Students Selected : 1

  • Position Offered: Software Engineer
    Capgemini India visited for the third time and recruited 14 students this year. It was a two day placement process.
    1. Online Aptitude Test
    2. Group Discussion
    3. Technical Round and Result Announcement
    All B-Tech and M-Tech (CSE) students were allowed to sit.
    No. Of Students Selected : 14
